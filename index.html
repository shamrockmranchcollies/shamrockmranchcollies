Welcome to Shamrock M Ranch

We live on a 2½ acre ranch on beautiful Camano Island — about an hour north of Seattle and an hour south of the Canadian border. Our collies share our home, our land, and our lives alongside our horses, and we consider every one of them family.

For 25 years, we have spent countless hours handpicking beautiful, healthy collies — avoiding inbreeding and line-breeding — because we believe that's what matters most when raising exceptional family companions.

Every dog in our program has been chosen with intention, heart, and a deep love for this breed. We invite you to meet our dogs, explore our upcoming litter, and join the growing community of Shamrock M Ranch families.

About Shamrock M Ranch

We live on a 2½ acre ranch on beautiful Camano Island in Washington State — about an hour north of Seattle and an hour south of the Canadian border. Our collies are raised alongside our horses and are very much a part of our family life, not just our breeding program.

My parents raised and bred collies while I was growing up, and when I started my own family I carried on that tradition. For over 25 years, right here on this same piece of land, collies have been at the heart of our home.

Our Commitment to Health

All of our adult dogs have been eye-checked for CEA (Collie Eye Anomaly) and have healthy eyes. We have spent years carefully selecting dogs that avoid the inbreeding and line-breeding so common in the breed — because we believe that matters most when it comes to raising healthy, happy family pets.

We never make demands that you show your collie. We only require that you care for and love your dog like family.

Our Dogs & Puppies

Our collies are show quality, with excellent temperament, movement, and coat. They meet the American Collie Club Illustrated Standard. Puppies are placed with Limited AKC registration (spay/neuter agreement), though Full AKC is available to approved homes for an additional fee. All puppies leave us current on vaccinations and worming with a written health guarantee.

Our Community

Over 25 years, we've built something we're truly proud of — not just a breeding program, but a community. Our Facebook page is home to past and present Shamrock M Ranch families sharing their lives with their collies. We love seeing those updates, and we know you will too.

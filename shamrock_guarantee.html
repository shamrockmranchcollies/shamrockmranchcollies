Our Collie Promise

Raising collies with love, care & decades of dedication to the breed

01  ·  HEALTH

Certified Healthy at Departure

Every puppy leaves with age-appropriate vaccinations, bi-weekly de-worming, a complete health record, and AKC eligibility.

02  ·  GUARANTEE

One-Year Congenital Guarantee

We stand behind our bloodlines with a full one-year guarantee against fatal or life-altering congenital conditions originating prenatally.

03  ·  TEMPERAMENT

Sound Mind & Spirit

Each puppy is raised in a loving ranch environment and guaranteed to be of sound temperament at the time of sale.

04  ·  SAFETY

Collie Drug Sensitivity Guidance

We provide every buyer with guidance on collie-sensitive medications including Ivermectin and Imodium — protecting your dog for life.

05  ·  SUPPORT

Lifelong Breeder Support

We remain a resource for every family we place a puppy with. Questions, concerns, or just a puppy update — we are always here.

06  ·  PROTECTION

Every Dog Comes Home

No Shamrock M Ranch collie will ever end up in a shelter. We are always first call if life changes — we will find them a wonderful home.

All correspondence is handled by email to ensure every communication is documented for your protection and ours.
